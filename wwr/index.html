We offer different outdoors.

On the Nile River we raft a 10km section of river with a short break along the way.
During the break we will learn about the Indigenous Culture, the Famous Nile River Sockeye Salmon Run and we will go cliff jumping into the Nile River We will be padding through the Tsutswecw Provincial Park for 1hr 10 minutes.

During the summer months we encourage family groups to join us with kids aged 7 and up.
July and August is a great time to raft on the Nile with warm river temperatures reaching up to 25 degrees celsius!
Lots of opportunities for swimming along side the raft as we drift down the river and the rapids are at a fun but manageable level for the younger kids.
During the Spring flows in June we encourage the experienced rafter or you first time thrill seekers to join us. Water levels in June are higher which means bigger rapids and lots of fun. Water temperatures can be colder during spring flows so we provide each guest with a wet suit and fleece jacket.
