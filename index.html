Benue State

Benue state is in north-central Nigeria. It is bordered by Kogi state to the west, Taraba to the east, CrossRiver to the South, and Nasarawa to the north. It is inhabited predominantly by the Tiv, Idoma and Igede people. Minority ethnic groups in Benue are Etulo, Igbo and Jukun people etc. Its capital is Makurdi.
